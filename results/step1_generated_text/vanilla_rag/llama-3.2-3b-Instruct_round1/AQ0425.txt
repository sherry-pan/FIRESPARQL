Question:
Give me a list of research papers along with their titles and IDs, that have performed benchmarks on the WMT2016 German-English dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
 PREFIX orkg: <http://orkg.org/orkg/>
     SELECT?title?id
     WHERE {
    ?paper orkg:HAS_BENCHMARK?benchmark.
    ?benchmark orkg:HAS_DATASET <http://orkg.org/dataset/WMT2016-DE-EN>.
    ?paper orkg:CSVW_Titles?title.
    ?benchmark orkg:HAS_METHOD?method.
    ?paper orkg:HAS_ID?id.
     FILTER (REGEX(STR(?title), "WMT2016") || REGEX(STR(?method), "WMT2016"))
     }
