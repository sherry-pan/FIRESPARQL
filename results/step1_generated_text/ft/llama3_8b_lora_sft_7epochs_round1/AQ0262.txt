Question:
Can you list the models that have been evaluated on the Atari 2600 Skiing dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
 SELECT DISTINCT?model?model_lbl
     WHERE {
      ?dataset        a                orkgc:Dataset;
                       rdfs:label      ?dataset_lbl.
       FILTER (str(?dataset_lbl) = "Atari 2600 Skiing")
      ?benchmark      orkgp:HAS_DATASET      ?dataset.
      ?cont        ?benchmark
      ?model       ?cont;
                     orkgp:P31           ?implementation.
      ?implementation    orkgp:P30           ?dataset.
      ?model           orkgp:P32           ?model_lbl.
     }<|eot_id|>
