Question:
What are the metrics of evaluation over the OntoNotes dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
 SELECT?metric?metric_lbl (COUNT(?evaluators) AS?evaluators)
      WHERE {
        orkgr:R23451 orkgp:compareContribution?cont;
                  orkgp:P4312?evaluators.
       ?cont orkgp:HAS_DATASET?dataset.
       ?dataset orkgp:P4311?metric.
        OPTIONAL {?evaluators orkgp:P4313?evaluators_lbl.}
       ?metric rdfs:label?metric_lbl.
      } GROUP BY?metric?metric_lbl; ORDER BY DESC.      LIMIT 1;      ```
