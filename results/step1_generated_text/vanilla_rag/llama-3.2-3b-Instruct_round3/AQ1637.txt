Question:
What is the name of the top performing model in terms of Score score when benchmarked on the Cart Pole (OpenAI Gym) dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
 PREFIX orkg: <http://orkg.org/orkg/>
     PREFIX rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#>
     SELECT?model 
     WHERE {
    ?model orkg:hasMethod?m.
    ?m orkg:hasDataset "Cart Pole (OpenAI Gym)".
    ?m orkg:hasBenchmark?b.
    ?b orkg:hasScore?s.
    ?s orkg:hasNumber?n.
    ?n orkg:hasNumber?n.
    ?m orkg:hasApproach?a.
    ?a rdfs:label?name.
     FILTER (CONTAINS(LCASE(?name), "Cart Pole") OR CONTAINS(LCASE(?name), "CartPole"))
     }
