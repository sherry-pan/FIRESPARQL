Question:
Indicate the model that performed best in terms of Score metric on the Atari 2600 Asteroids benchmark dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
PREFIX orkg: <http://orkg.org/orkg/ontology/>
PREFIX rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#>

SELECT?model?score
WHERE {
 ?model a orkg:Method.
 ?model orkg:HAS_EVALUATION?evaluation.
 ?evaluation orkg:HAS_METRIC?metric.
 ?metric rdfs:label "Score".
 ?model orkg:HAS_DATASET?dataset.
 ?dataset orkg:HAS_BENCHMARK?benchmark.
 ?benchmark rdfs:label "Atari 2600 Asteroids".
 ?model orkg:HAS_BEST_SCORE?score.
}<|eot_id|>
