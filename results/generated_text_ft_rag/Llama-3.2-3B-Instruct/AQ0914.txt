Question:
Can you list the metrics used to evaluate models on the Barabasi-Albert dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
 SELECT?label
     WHERE {
      ?dataset       a                orkgc:Dataset;
                      rdfs:label      ?dataset_lbl.
           FILTER (str(?dataset_lbl) = "Barabasi-Albert")
         ?benchmark      orkgp:HAS_DATASET      ?dataset;
                           orkgp:HAS_EVALUATION   ?eval.
         ?paper         orkgp:P31               ?eval;
                         rdfs:label              ?eval_lbl.
         ?cont         orkgp:P31               ?paper;
                        rdfs:label              ?cont_lbl.
         ?metric       rdfs:label              ?cont_lbl.
         ?eval         orkgp:P31               ?cont;
                         orkgp:HAS_METRIC      ?metric.
          BIND(xsd:integer(5) AS?cont_num)
         ?cont      orkgp:HAS_CONTRIB        ?cont_num;
                     orkgp:P32               ?cont.
         ?contrib      orkgp:P31               ?cont;
                        rdfs:label              ?contrib_lbl.
          BIND(xsd:integer(5) AS?contrib_num)
         ?contrib      orkgp:HAS_METRIC        ?metric;
                        orkgp:HAS_EVALUATION     ?eval.
         ?cont_metric    orkgp:P31               ?contrib;
                          orkgp:HAS_METRIC        ?metric.
          OPTIONAL {?cont_metric      orkgp:P32               ?cont_eval.
                   ?cont_eval          orkgp:HAS_METRIC        ?metric.
                   ?cont_eval          rdfs:label              ?cont_eval_lbl.
                    BIND(xsd:integer(5) AS?cont_eval_num)
                   ?cont_eval      orkgp:HAS_EVALUATION     ?cont_eval_num.}
      ORDER BY (ASC(?cont_eval_num))
      LIMIT 5.
