Question:
Provide a list of research paper titles and IDs that have benchmarked models on the Kinetics-600 dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
PREFIX orkg: <http://orkg.org/orkg/>
PREFIX csvw: <http://www.w3.org/ns/csvw>

SELECT?paperTitle?paperID
WHERE {
 ?paper orkg:HAS_EVALUATION?evaluation.
 ?evaluation orkg:HAS_DATASET?dataset.
 ?dataset csvw:datasetName "Kinetics-600".
 ?paper orkg:CSVW_Titles?paperTitle.
 ?paper orkg:CSVW_Number?paperID.
}<|eot_id|>
