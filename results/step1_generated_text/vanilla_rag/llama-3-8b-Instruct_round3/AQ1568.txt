Question:
Which model has achieved the highest F1 score on the CoNLL 2003 (English) benchmark dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
PREFIX orkg: <http://orkg.org/orkg/>
PREFIX rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#>

SELECT?model?f1_score
WHERE {
 ?model a orkg:Model.
 ?model orkg:HAS_EVALUATION?evaluation.
 ?evaluation orkg:HAS_METRIC?metric.
 ?metric rdfs:label?f1_score.
 ?model orkg:HAS_METHOD?method.
 ?method rdfs:label?method_label.
 ?model orkg:HAS_BENCHMARK?benchmark.
 ?benchmark rdfs:label "CoNLL 2003 (English)".
 ?model orkg:HAS_DATASET?dataset.
 ?dataset rdfs:label "CoNLL 2003".
  FILTER (?f1_score = MAX(?f1_score))
}<|eot_id|>
