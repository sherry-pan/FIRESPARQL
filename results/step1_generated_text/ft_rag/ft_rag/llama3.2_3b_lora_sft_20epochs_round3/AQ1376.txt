Question:
Which model has achieved the highest F1 score score on the Penn Treebank benchmark dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
 SELECT?model?model_lbl
     WHERE {
      ?dataset       a                orkgc:Dataset;
                      rdfs:label      ?dataset_lbl.
       FILTER (str(?dataset_lbl = "Penn Treebank")
               || str(?dataset_lbl = "Penn Treebank (development)"))
      ?benchmark      orkgp:HAS_DATASET      ?dataset;
                      orkgp:HAS_EVALUATION   ?eval.
      ?eval           orkgp:HAS_VALUE        ?value;
                      orkgp:P71080         ?micro_f1.
       BIND(xsd:float(?value) AS?micro_f1_value)
      ?cont         orkgp:HAS_BENCHMARK     ?benchmark;
                      orkgp:HAS_METHOD       ?method.
      ?method      orkgp:P97142          ?cont.
      ?model      orkgp:P71103          ?method;
                  rdfs:label              ?model_lbl.
       ORDER BY DESC(?micro_f1_value)
       LIMIT 1
     }<|eot_id|>
