Question:
Provide a list of research paper titles and IDs that have benchmarked models on the SciERC dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
PREFIX orkg: <http://openresearchknowledge.org/orkg/>
PREFIX owl: <http://www.w3.org/2002/07/owl#>
PREFIX rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#>
PREFIX xsd: <http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#>

SELECT?paperTitle?paperId
WHERE {
   ?paper a orkg:ResearchPaper.
   ?paper orkg:hasBenchmarkedModel?benchmarkedModel.
   ?benchmarkedModel orkg:hasDataset?dataset.
   ?dataset rdfs:label "SciERC".
   ?paper orkg:hasTitle?paperTitle.
   ?paper orkg:hasId?paperId.
}<|eot_id|>
