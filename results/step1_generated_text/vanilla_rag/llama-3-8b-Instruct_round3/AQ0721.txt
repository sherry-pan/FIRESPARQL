Question:
What evaluation metrics are commonly used when benchmarking models on the DRI Corpus dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
PREFIX orkg: <http://orkg.org/orkg/>
PREFIX rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#>

SELECT?evaluationMetric
WHERE {
 ?paper a orkg:Paper;
         orkg:HAS_EVALUATION?evaluationMetric.
 ?evaluationMetric a orkg:EvaluationMetric;
                    rdfs:label?metricLabel.
  FILTER (?metricLabel CONTAINS "DRI Corpus" )
}<|eot_id|>
