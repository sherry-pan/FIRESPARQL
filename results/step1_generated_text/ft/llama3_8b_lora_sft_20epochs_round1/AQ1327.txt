Question:
What is the highest benchmark result achieved on the IMDb-M dataset, including the metric and its value?

Generated SPARQL:
 SELECT DISTINCT?metric?metric_lbl (MAX(?value) AS?score)
     WHERE {
       orkgr:R70642 orkgp:compareContribution?contrib.
      ?contrib orkgp:P34?benchmark.
      ?benchmark rdfs:label?benchmark_lbl.
       FILTER(REGEX(STR(?benchmark_lbl), "IMDb-M")
      ?contrib orkgp:P3004?metric.
      ?metric rdfs:label?metric_lbl.
      ?contrib orkgp:P3005?value.
     }
     ORDER BY DESC(?score)<|eot_id|>
