Question:
What is the highest benchmark result achieved on the Cartpole, swingup (DMControl500k) dataset, including the metric and its value?

Generated SPARQL:
 PREFIX orkg: <http://www.orkg.org/>
     PREFIX dcterms: <http://purl.org/dc/terms/>
     PREFIX prov: <http://www.w3.org/ns/prov#>
     SELECT?metric?value
     WHERE {
      ?paper a orkg:Paper.
      ?paper dcterms:title?title.
       FILTER (STRSTARTS(?title, "Cartpole, swingup (DMControl500k)")).
      ?paper prov:wasGeneratedBy?run.
      ?run orkg:benchmarkResult?result.
      ?result orkg:metric?metric.
      ?result orkg:value?value.
       FILTER (?metric!= "time".
       FILTER (?value = MAX(?value))
     }<|eot_id|>
