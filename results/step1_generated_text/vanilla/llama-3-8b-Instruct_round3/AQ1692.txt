Question:
Indicate the model that performed best in terms of Score metric on the Atari 2600 Crazy Climber benchmark dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
 PREFIX orkg: <http://www.openresearchknowledgegraph.org/ontology/>
     PREFIX dcterms: <http://purl.org/dc/terms/>
     PREFIX owl: <http://www.w3.org/2002/07/owl#>
     SELECT?model?score
     WHERE {
    ?model a orkg:Model ;
     orkg:hasBenchmark?benchmark.
    ?benchmark a orkg:Atari2600CrazyClimberBenchmark ;
     dcterms:identifier "CrazyClimber" ;
     orkg:hasResult?result.
    ?result a orkg:Result ;
     orkg:metric "Score" ;
     orkg:hasValue?score.
     FILTER (?score = orkg:bestValue)
     }<|eot_id|>
