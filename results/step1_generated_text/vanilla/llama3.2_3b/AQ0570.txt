Question:
What are the titles and IDs of research papers that include a benchmark for the WSC dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
 PREFIX orkg: <http://orkg.org/ontology/>
     PREFIX wsc: <http://orkg.org/ontology/wsc/>
     PREFIX wsd: <http://orkg.org/ontology/wsd/>
     SELECT?paper?id
     WHERE {
      ?paper a orkg:Paper.
       wsc:includesBenchmark?benchmark.
       wsc:dataset wsd:WSC.
      ?benchmark wsd:benchmarkFor?paper.
      ?benchmark wsd:dataset?dataset.
       FILTER (?dataset = wsd:WSC)
      ?paper wsc:hasTitle?title.
      ?paper orkg:id?id
       FILTER (STR(?title)!= "")
     } 
     LIMIT 10 
     ORDER BY (DESC(?paper orkg:year))
