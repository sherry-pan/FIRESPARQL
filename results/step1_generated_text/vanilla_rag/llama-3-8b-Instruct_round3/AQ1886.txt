Question:
Provide a list of benchmarked datasets related to the Sentence Classification research area?

Generated SPARQL:
PREFIX orkg: <http://orkg.org/orkg/>
PREFIX rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#>

SELECT?dataset
WHERE {
 ?dataset a orkg:Dataset.
 ?dataset orkg:HAS_BENCHMARK?benchmark.
 ?benchmark a orkg:Benchmark.
  FILTER(?benchmark orkg:HAS_EVALUATION?evaluation.
 ?evaluation rdfs:label?evaluationLabel.
  FILTER(LANG(?evaluationLabel) = "en" && CONTAINS(?evaluationLabel, "Sentence Classification")).
}<|eot_id|>
