Question:
What are the titles and IDs of research papers that include a benchmark for the DCASE dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
 PREFIX orkg: <http://orkg.org/orkg/predicate/>
     SELECT?title?id 
     WHERE {
    ?paper orkg:has_dataset <http://orkg.org/orkg/ontology/DCASE>.
    ?paper orkg:has_benchmark?benchmark.
    ?benchmark orkg:titles?title.
    ?benchmark orkg:number?id.
     }<|eot_id|>
