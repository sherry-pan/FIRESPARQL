Question:
What are the titles and IDs of research papers that include a benchmark for the TempEval-3 dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
 PREFIX orkg: <http://orkg.org/orkg/predicate/>
     SELECT?title?id
     WHERE {
    ?paper orkg:has_dataset?dataset.
    ?dataset orkg:has_benchmark?benchmark.
    ?benchmark orkg:has_title?title.
    ?benchmark orkg:has_number?id.
     FILTER (bif:contains(?dataset, "TempEval-3") || bif:contains(?id, "TempEval-3"))
     }
    <|eot_id|>
