Question:
What is the best performing model benchmarking the X-Sum dataset in terms of ROUGE-2 metric?

Generated SPARQL:
 PREFIX orkg: <http://www.openresearchknowledgegraph.org/ontology/>
    PREFIX xsd: <http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#>
    PREFIX dc: <http://purl.org/dc/elements/1.1/>
    SELECT?model?score
    WHERE {
     ?model a orkg:Model.
     ?model orkg:benchmarks?benchmark.
     ?benchmark a orkg:Benchmark.
     ?benchmark orkg:dataset <http://www.openresearchknowledgegraph.org/dataset/X-Sum>.
     ?benchmark orkg:metric orkg:ROUGE-2.
     ?benchmark orkg:score?score.
      FILTER (?score = FILTER(ORDER BY DESC(?score)))
      LIMIT 1
    }<|eot_id|>
