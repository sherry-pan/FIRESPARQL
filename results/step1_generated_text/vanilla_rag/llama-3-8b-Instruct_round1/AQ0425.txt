Question:
Give me a list of research papers along with their titles and IDs, that have performed benchmarks on the WMT2016 German-English dataset?

Generated SPARQL:
 PREFIX orkg: <http://orkg.org/orkg/ontology/>
     PREFIX csvw: <http://www.w3.org/ns/csvw#>

     SELECT?paper?title?id
     WHERE {
      ?paper a orkg:ResearchPaper.
      ?paper orkg:HAS_EVALUATION?evaluation.
      ?evaluation orkg:HAS_BENCHMARK?benchmark.
      ?benchmark orkg:HAS_DATASET <http://wmt2016.org/dataset/de-en>.
      ?paper csvw:CSVW_Titles?title.
      ?paper orkg:ID?id.
     }<|eot_id|>
